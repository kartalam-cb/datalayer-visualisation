## Color Scheme

### Toolbar
- **Background**: Light gray (#f5f5f5)
- **Buttons**: Blue primary buttons, gray secondary, red danger
- **GTM Active**: Green pulsing dot with animation

### Events Panel (Left)
- **Background**: White (#ffffff)
- **Selected Event**: Blue background (#d2e3fc) with blue left border (#4285f4)
- **Navigation Marker**: Yellow background (#fbbc04) with red left border (#ea4335)
- **Hover**: Light blue (#e8f0fe)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#202124)
- **Meta Text**: Medium gray (#5f6368)
- **URL Text**: Light gray (#80868b)

### Event Details (Right)
- **Background**: White (#ffffff)
- **Key (Property)**: Purple (#881280)
- **String Value**: Blue (#1a1aa6)
- **Number Value**: Green (#164)
- **Boolean Value**: Dark Blue (#219)
- **Null Value**: Purple Italic (#708)
- **Brackets**: Gray (#444)
- **Network Section**: Light gray background (#f5f5f5)

### Network Badges
- **GA4**: Green badge (#34a853)
- **UA**: Green badge (#34a853)
- **GTM**: Green badge (#34a853)
- **Pending**: Yellow badge (#fbbc04)
- **Failed**: Red badge (#ea4335)
